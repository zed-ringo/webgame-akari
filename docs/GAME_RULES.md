# Akari Lanterns Game Rules

Last updated: 2026-05-06

## Goal

Place lamps so every white cell is lit.

## Rules

- Lamps illuminate horizontally and vertically until blocked by a wall.
- Lamps may not see each other along a row or column.
- Numbered black cells require exactly that many adjacent lamps.
- Clear when all white cells are lit and no rule is violated.

## Controls

- Tap/click a white cell to toggle a lamp.
- Hint highlights an unlit cell to investigate.
- Difficulty buttons switch fixed puzzles.
